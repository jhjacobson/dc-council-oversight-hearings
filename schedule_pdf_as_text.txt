ABBREVIATED NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARINGS
AGENCY PERFORMANCE OVERSIGHT HEARINGS
FISCAL YEAR 2023-2024
1/9/2024
SUMMARY
January 17, 2024 to Agency Performance Oversight Hearings on Fiscal Year
March 1, 2024 2023-2024
February 1, 2024 Committee of the Whole Public Briefing on the Fiscal Year
2023 Annual Comprehensive Financial Report (ACFR) at
11:00 a.m. in Room 500
The Council of the District of Columbia hereby gives notice of its intention to hold public oversight
hearings on agency performance for FY 2023 and FY 2024. The hearings will begin Monday, January
17, 2024 and conclude on Friday, March 1, 2024.
Please refer to the specific instructions, found at the end of this notice, on how each Committee will
operate their hearings. Some hearings listed in this notice will be broadcast live on DC Council Channel
13 and streamed live at www.dccouncil.gov and entertainment.dc.gov, while others will be streamed live
at the link provided.
Witnesses who anticipate needing language interpretation, or require sign language interpretation, are
requested to inform the relevant Committee office of the need as soon as possible, but no later than five
(5) business days before the proceeding. We will make every effort to fulfill timely requests, however
requests received in fewer than five (5) business days may not be fulfilled and alternatives may be
offered.
